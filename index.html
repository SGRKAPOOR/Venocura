PICC Line

Peripherally Inserted Central Catheter

Neonatal PICC Lines

Venocura PICC lines are designed for prolonged central venous access in neonates — including extremely low birthweight infants. The catheter range accommodates the anatomical and clinical requirements of NICU practice.

1 Fr — Single Lumen 2 Fr — Single Lumen With Stylet Without Stylet

Silicone construction with high radiopacity for reliable tip confirmation on X-ray

Soft, flexible tip profile designed to reduce the risk of vessel irritation during insertion

Depth markings at regular intervals for accurate insertion length monitoring

Stylet available for guided insertion in compromised peripheral access scenarios

Sterile, single-use packaging with extended shelf-life stability

Clinical Indication

Suitable for prolonged parenteral nutrition, medication administration, and fluid therapy in neonates where peripheral IV access is insufficient or impractical.

Umbilical Catheter

Umbilical Venous & Arterial Catheter

Umbilical Catheters

Venocura umbilical catheters facilitate arterial and venous access through the umbilical vessels in the immediate neonatal period — a critical access route for resuscitation, monitoring, and therapeutic interventions.

Single Lumen Double Lumen Umbilical Arterial (UAC) Umbilical Venous (UVC)

Radio-opaque line along full catheter length for precise positioning confirmation

Smooth, atraumatic tip to minimise vessel wall contact during catheter placement

Double lumen configuration enables simultaneous infusion and blood sampling

Luer lock connector with graduated measurement markings for depth control

Formulated for extended indwell, subject to clinical protocol guidelines

Clinical Indication

Indicated for emergency vascular access in the delivery room and NICU admission phase, exchange transfusion, continuous haemodynamic monitoring, and total parenteral nutrition in term and preterm neonates.

Arterial Line

Peripheral Arterial Catheter

Arterial Lines

Venocura arterial catheters provide reliable peripheral arterial access for continuous blood pressure monitoring and repeated arterial blood gas sampling — essential in neonates requiring ventilatory support or haemodynamic management.

Peripheral Arterial Continuous BP Monitoring ABG Sampling

Short-bevel needle with fluoropolymer catheter for smooth arterial cannulation in small-calibre neonatal vessels

Flash-back chamber provides immediate blood confirmation on successful arterial puncture

Transparent catheter material allows continuous visual monitoring of blood column

Luer slip and luer lock connectors compatible with standard pressure monitoring sets

Clinical Indication

Indicated for continuous invasive arterial pressure monitoring and repeated arterial blood gas sampling in neonates on mechanical ventilation, high-frequency oscillation, or with haemodynamic instability requiring intensive monitoring.

Our Story

A Brand Born From Clinical Purpose

Venocura is the vascular access range of Life Opto, a medical device company focused on neonatal and critical care. The Venocura name was born from a commitment to go beyond what a catheter technically needs to do — and build products that clinicians can genuinely trust.

In the NICU, a vascular access device is not just a product. It is part of a treatment plan. It is in contact with a patient who may weigh less than 500 grams. Its performance — or failure — has direct clinical consequences.

That understanding shapes every decision: material selection, tip geometry, flow optimisation, packaging, and pricing. Each consideration is filtered through the lens of clinical relevance and patient safety.

Our Mission

To provide NICUs across India with world-class neonatal vascular access products — combining clinical excellence with accessibility, so that every neonate has access to the standard of care they deserve.
